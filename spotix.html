What is spotix?

According to Spotix, smart-photographers are photographers who take photos with their smartphones. They are the future of photography, especially with the growth of Instagram nowadays.

Spotix is an app where photographers can share their amazing photos, interact with passionate community and particiate to photographer contests everyday through our gamification process.

Gamification

Gamification is an important part of the project. Indeed, one of the requirements from the brief was to create a user experience based on gamification.

Our vision of it was to allow users to participate to contests. There are several themes available opened by Spotix moderators.

Users can upload their photographs or choose a photo from their portfolios (profile) and participate to the contest. To win a contest and a trophy, your photo simply has to collect the higher number of likes from other participants.

Users can also unlock badges like "you won 3 gold trohies" or "you have 400 followers" during their app usage.

Design elements

The app design is quite minimalist with rounded shapes for buttons and images. The navigation menu is very simple: Home - Contests - Tips - Portfolio.

Home

Two feeds are available: one to see posts from people you follow and one from trendy posts.

Portfolio

Public portfolio allows users to share and store their best photographs. From this portfolio users can add new photos and access their numbers of followers, trophies and badges.

Tips

The tips section gives access to information and advice about photography. Users receives personnalised tips according to the type of phone they own.

Contest

Smart-photographers can participate to photography contest directly inside the app. They can also display previous contests and know if they won or not.
