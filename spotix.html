What is spotix?

According to Spotix, smart-photographers (photographers who take photos with their smartphones) are the future of photography, especially with the growth of instagram nowadays.

Spotix is an app where photographers can share their amazing photos, interact with passionate community and particiate to photographer contests everyday through gamificatioin.

Gamification

There is also an important part of the app which is the gamification. Indeed, one of the requirements from the brief was to create a user experience based on gamification.

Our vision of it was to allow users to participate to contests. There are several themes available opened by Spotix moderators. Thus, users can upload their photo or take a photo from their portfolio (profile) and participate to the contest.

To win a contest, your photo simply has to collect the higher number of likes from other participants.

Design elements

Home

Two feeds are available : one to watch posts from people you follow and one from trendy post or from people with same interests as you.

Portfolio

Public portfolio allows users to share and store their best photographs. From this portfolio they can add new photos and access their numbers of followers, trophies and badges.

Tips

The app give access to tips and information about photography. Users receives personnalised tips according to their phone

Contest

Smart-photographers can participate to photography contest directly in side the app. They can also see previsous contest and know if they've won or not.
