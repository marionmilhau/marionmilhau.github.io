Briefing

Le rucher du coeur d'Hérault is a company that sells bees and hives to profesionnals (beekeepers and farmers) and non- profesionnals. Customers will be able to purshase a range of products from the queen bee to an entire hive.

They wanted a logo that remind the modern and terroir aspects of the company. Modern because they are experts and they use modern technologies and technics in their process. Terroir because they are close to their beautiful region, the South of France, and to their customers as well.

Creation process

My logo creation process is separated in 6 steps:

1. Creating a moodboard
After getting the briefing from my client, I usually feel this atmosphere they want to create for their branding. I inspired myself on pinterest or google image. I created 3 moodboards: one for logos, one for colors and one with words.

2. Sketching
When I get an idea of the branding univers, I started sketching on paper any ideas that came to mind. I tried to not put any judgement on my scketches and keep my ideas on only one idea.

3. Digital sketching
I selected several logos that I liked on paper and made them as vectors on Illustrator. It gave a way better idea of what it would look like once finished. I made some changes here and there and elaborated 4 logos.

4. Presentation
I made a presentation on InDesign for each logo with their version on a light background, their version on a dark background and a short description of what this logo was representing and why it fits with the briefing. Then, the client can choose which one they prefer.

5. Adjustments
Sometimes, the client will want to make some changes on the logo they chose, that was the case here. We changed the color of the thym branch for a green one and made a version with the branch inside the hexagon.

6. File export
The last step of the process is the file export of your logo. Depending on what you agreed with your client in the quote, I'll need to export several files.

For this project, I exported the logo in their normal version, white version and dark version. Each one in .png with transparent background, .jpeg which can be used as social media thumbnail and .eps which is the vectorized version.
